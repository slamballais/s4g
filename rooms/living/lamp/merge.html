You enter a room and find a singular note, right in the middle.
It explains how you should build the combined key of the living room.
Basically, there are four keys that are ordered.
They are obtained from the television (1), the white sofa (2), the green sofa (3), and the fireplace (4).
This should create a new number consisting of 8 digits.
To use the key, you have to take the 8 digits, and place it at the end of the URL of a room that requires a combined key.
Keep in mind, though... There are multiple rooms that require combined keys.
So, this key may not work where you think it will...
