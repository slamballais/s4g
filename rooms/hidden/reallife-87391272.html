After hours in the elevator, you arrive at your destination: the basement.
It's not your typical, human basement.
A thick, dense mist covers all surfaces, glowing with a pulsating, deep red light that seems to throb with life.
The floor feels soft beneath your feet, like stepping on layers of forgotten memories, or perhaps long-decayed remnants of those who came before you.
You hear thousands of screams of agony, although it is impossible to say where they come from.
The air itself is suffocating, heavy with despair, as if every breath pulls you deeper into a madness you didn’t know you carried.
Strange shapes writhe in the corners of your vision, only to vanish when you turn to face them.
Above you, the sky twists in unnatural spirals, pulling all light into a yawning void at its center—an abyss that watches, waiting.
As you walk further, the sound of whispers grows louder.
They speak in tongues forgotten by time, but the message is clear in your mind:

You should not be here.

Yet, you cannot turn back.
You feel it, don't you?
The pull of something beyond fear, beyond reason.
Something ancient and unfathomable that has waited for you to arrive.
Your heart pounds faster as the ground trembles beneath you.
It's not the earth, but the very fabric of reality, unraveling as you move deeper into this hellish underworld.
Four doors materialize from the mist, each one more twisted and grotesque than the last.
Each door contains a puzzle, but these aren't meant to be solved by you... they exist to break you.
As the whispers grow more intense, you slowly start to realise that this is as far as you may get.

Where do you want to go?
