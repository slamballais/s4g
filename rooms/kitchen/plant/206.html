You enter a room and find a singular note, right in the middle.
It explains how you should build the combined key of the kitchen.
Basically, there are four keys that are ordered.
They are obtained from the oven (1), the fire alarm (2), the kitchen drawer (3), and the pantry (4).
Each of those contain song lyrics. For each, you should grab the 10th word (lower case).
To use the key, paste the four words behind each other, and place them at the end of the URL of a room that requires a combined key.
Keep in mind, though... There are multiple rooms that require combined keys.
So, this key may not work where you think it will...
