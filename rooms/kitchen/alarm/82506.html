You enter a room that is just piles upon piles of books.
Some books look familiar, like Jane Eyre and Gulliver's Travels.
But a lot of other books do not make any sense at all...
"Dancing Umbrellas in the Moonlight Sandwich"?
"When Clouds Become Pineapple Smoothies"?
The pages mostly contain random words...
It seems that the piles and piles of books contains real books and fake books.
You spend the next 10 years of your life compiling all the ISBN-10 codes on the books.
If you've never read a book: the ISBN-10 code is a unique identifier for a book.
It looks like this: 0881034207
You ended up storing all those ISBN-10 codes into the `isbn.rds` file.
Now, your job is to find how many ISBN-10 codes are actually real.
Look at Wikipedia to see how to find real ISBN-10 codes:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISBN#ISBN-10_check_digits
The number of real ISBN-10 codes is the new hidden word.
